About Tires On Rent



Every big revolution begins with a
tiny spark of inspiration.

And that’s how Tires On Rent was born.

For months together, we observed that a good number of students and financially challenged individuals had to put up with worn-out tires that were potentially risky – endangering their lives and the lives of those travelling with them. The reason was simple – unaffordability.

Our prolonged and deeper research revealed another fascinating side of the picture.
Many wealthy people, blissfully unaware of the plight of the financially weaker segment, were literally discarding their moderately used tires or selling them for ridiculously low prices to exploitative buyers.


Then, out of the blue, came that little spark of an idea.

What if we could bring the tire seekers and tire owners together in one common platform?
The tire seekers could enjoy the double benefit of affordability and safety.

And the tire owners could profit from those spare tires, besides of course deriving the satisfaction of serving someone in need.

That was how Tires On Rent (TOR) was born.

A Canadian based online classified ads portal, TOR is the most convenient and the most innovative platform that links tire seekers with tire owners.

We, the founders of TOR, strive to create a better, safer and a happier world.

Well, we are on a roll in our journey of success and we are sure to implement more path-breaking features to further delight our customers.
